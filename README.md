## Calling All Zebras

**A nonprofit website for teens with chronic or invisible illnesses, offering community, support, and educational resources.**

## Purpose

Calling All Zebras aims to provide teens living with chronic or invisible illnesses a safe and supportive online space. The website offers:

- Community stories and peer support  
- Educational resources about different conditions  
- Guidance for coping and navigating daily life  
- Opportunities to donate and support the nonprofit
